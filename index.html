Is this for you?

This journal is for you if...

  • You sometimes react in ways that surprise even yourself
  • You keep repeating the same patterns in relationships and don't know why
  • You hear a critical inner voice that you can't seem to quiet
  • You find it hard to ask for help, say no, or express what you need
  • You want to understand yourself better — not fix yourself, understand yourself
  • You're ready to be honest with yourself, one page at a time
